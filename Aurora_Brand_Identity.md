# Aurora HY Brand Identity V1

## Keywords
Premium, Luxury, Professional, Confidence, Precision, Future, Trust, Calm.

Avoid: Cyberpunk, Gaming, Crypto, Neon RGB, Template.

## Symbol
The Aurora Symbol combines three ideas:
- Aurora flow: calm directional motion.
- North Star: precision, navigation, trust.
- Flow core: controlled intelligence without using letters or a generic infinity mark.

## Colors
- Background: `#060B17`
- Surface: `#0B1325`
- Primary: `#4CC9FF`
- Secondary: `#7A5CFF`
- Gold: `#E8C547`
- White: `#F8FAFC`

## Typography
- Hero: 112px / Extra Bold
- Section: 72px / Extra Bold
- Card: 48px / Extra Bold
- Body: 18px
- Caption: 14px

## Motion
Allowed: fade, slide, glow, scale.
Forbidden: bounce, spin, shake.
